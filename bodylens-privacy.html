Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026
Soma is designed privacy-first. Your personal health data lives only in your browser — it never touches our servers.

Overview

We built Soma with a simple principle: your health data belongs to you. Body measurements, weight logs, training history, meal plans, and supplement records are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. We have no access to this data.

The only personal information we process is what is needed to authenticate your account.

Data we collect

When you sign in with Google via Supabase OAuth, we receive and store your Google account name and email address. This is used solely to identify your account and is held by Supabase in accordance with their privacy policy.

We do not collect, transmit, or store any health or body data on our servers.

Data we do not collect

The following data is never sent to or stored by Soma servers:

  • Body measurements and weight logs
  • Training history and session records
  • Meal plans and food logs
  • Supplement logs and stacks
  • Health goals and programme settings
  • Daily plan history and debrief notes

All of this data is stored in your browser's localStorage and never leaves your device.

AI processing

When you generate a plan, a summary of your goals and relevant metrics (such as target macros and training focus) is sent to the Anthropic API (Claude) to produce your plan. This transmission is stateless — Anthropic does not retain this data for model training.

We take care not to include personally identifiable information in these API calls. Your name and email address are not sent to Anthropic.

For more detail, see Anthropic's privacy policy.

Cookies and local storage

Soma uses your browser's localStorage and sessionStorage to store your profile, preferences, and generated plans. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics.

Supabase may set a session cookie to maintain your authentication state. No third-party advertising or behavioural tracking cookies are used.

Third-party services

Soma uses the following third-party services:

Supabase Authentication (Google OAuth) — Privacy policy
Anthropic AI plan generation (Claude) — Privacy policy
Google Fonts Typography — Privacy policy

Your rights

Because your health data lives in your browser, you are always in full control of it. You can delete all local data at any time by clearing your browser's storage for this site.

To delete your Supabase authentication record (your name and email), contact us via the feedback option in the app and we will process the deletion promptly.

Children

Soma is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used this service, please contact us so we can remove their account.

Contact

For questions or requests related to your privacy, please reach out via the feedback option within the app.
