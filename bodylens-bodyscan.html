How to use this
1
Open your scale app (Hume, InBody, Tanita, DEXA report) and take a screenshot or copy the numbers. Ask ChatGPT or Claude to summarise it — paste the screenshot and say “summarise my body composition results.”
2
Paste the summary below. Or just type the key numbers directly — weight, body fat %, lean mass, BMR if you have it. No special format needed.
3
BodyLens extracts the data, recalculates your targets from real measurements, and updates your coaching report.
