⚠ Who this is not for
BodyLens is built for metabolically healthy adults. It is not appropriate for people with Type 1 diabetes, Type 2 diabetes managed with insulin or sulphonylureas, reactive hypoglycaemia, or any condition requiring medically supervised glucose management. The fasting windows and meal timing in this programme can cause hypoglycaemia in people taking glucose-lowering medication. Full details →
